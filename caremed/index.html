A great medical team to help your needs

CareMed Enterprise Limited is a hospital chain based in London. It was founded in 1980 as the first corporate healthcare provider. Several of the CareMed's hospitals have been among the first in London to receive international healthcare accreditation by the America-based Joint Commission International (JCI) as well as 13 NABH National Accreditation Board for Hospitals & Healthcare Providers hospitals. In addition to providing hospitals, CareMed has developed services in telemedicine, after starting a pilot project in 1990 at Paris, founder's home village. CareMed signed a 'memorandum of understanding' with Health Education England in April 2017 to provide a large number of doctors to fill vacancies in the English National Health Service.
